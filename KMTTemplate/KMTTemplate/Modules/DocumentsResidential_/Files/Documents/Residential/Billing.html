- If no notes present ask if payment cleared customer's bank.

Payment - Toll-Free Bill Pay Phone Line


 

There is an alternate method for customers to pay their invoice, other than regular mail (lockbox) or Online Bill Pay:

· The Bill Pay Toll-Free number (877) 692-9729. This system will post payments within 24 business hours.

· Payment can still be made using EBPP , advise customer of this option for future payment use.

Payment – Account Closed/Written Off


 

1. Check division information page for Reinstatement/Write-Off Policy and follow that process.


 

2. Process payment on customer's EBPP account and key F2 CCPD for payment and F2 ADDM for debit of fee(s) and write-off amount.


Must collect:


§ All past due amounts (include all write-off amounts)

§ Reinstatement and/or collection fee (if applicable)

§ One quarter in advance for service, trash/recycling/yard waste/rental, if applicable (If customer has cart rental only, collect 1 yr in advance)

§ Cart redelivery fee (if applicable)


 

3. If payment is made, key F2 CSIN to reopen account.

 

- If payment will not process on customer's EBPP account then process payment on Division's Holding Account (9999991)

- Key closed F2 CSIN on customer's account


 

*Reminder-must place on CSR Memo field*:


  • Site Name
  • Site Address
  • Container Size
  • Acct# (if applies)

 

4. If customer informs you that they have made payment to a Collection Agency key F2 CCPD .

Auto Billing Authorization Agreement

 

 
Customer MUST complete a new form before account information can be changed by CSR.
-          Only change expiration date on current credit/debit cards.
 
Inform Customer that they can also log onto Online Bill Pay and update their own account information if that is a more convenient option.
·       If assisted customer with changes or mailing form to customer key F2 CSIN.
 
Refer to EBPP Quick Reference Guide for further questions or troubleshooting.
