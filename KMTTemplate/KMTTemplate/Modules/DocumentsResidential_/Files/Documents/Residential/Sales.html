New Service: Quote
 
If an Associate receives this call type; Get the caller’s Name, Phone number, and the service area and transfer the call to #3000017
 
Check:
A.      Divisions quote account (#7777777) for existing quote within the current month.
·         If there is a quote in the divisions quote account, proceed to step D.
 
B.      InfoPro for existing account (closed or open).
  • If OPEN account exists, verify if it’s the callers account.
-             If it is NOT the caller’s account, proceed to step C
  • If CLOSED account exists, verify if it’s the callers account.
-       If it is the caller’s account, and it has been closed in good standing, refer to Residential New Service: Sold
-       If it is the caller’s account, and it has been closed due to bad debt or written off, If previous account exists and was written off refer to "Payment - Closed/Written Off" call handling
 
C.      Review Division Service Area page for appropriate pricing page.
  • If you close the sale, follow the Residential New Service: Sold process.
 
**Do not complete or submit order form unless payment received during call**
 
  • If could not make sale, give the customer the quote number and inform them that it is valid for 24 hours only.
  • Key F2 CSIN on division quote account #7777777 and change the 'Schedule Completion' date for the last day of the current month, even if customer is mailing or making payment at division.
-  If making payment at division, follow process on Division Information page.
 
D.   If customer returns call and orders service, find F2 CSIN:
-  Review the pricing in the quote with the caller.
-  Change priority level to 'H'
-  Note "sold" in F2 body with CSR ID
-  Note the date of sale
-  Complete Residential Order Form.

Retention / Price Match

 

CS Associate:

Get the following information and transfer the call to a CS Specialist Ext# 300015

  • Caller’s Name
  • Phone number
  • Account #
  • Division #

 

CS Specialist / Consultant:

1. Review Division Service Area page for pricing and approved retention tactics.

·   If suggesting a decrease in service level to lower the customer’s price; follow the ‘Residential – Sales: Service Increase / Decrease’ process.

 

2. Negotiate rate using the SOS tactics.

·   Use the Residential Rate Adjustment calculator to determine the amount of the new rate (with the fees).

·   Inform the customer that the rates will be effective starting their next billing cycle.

o If the customer wants the rate rollback to be effective for the current invoice:

§ Use the Residential Rate Adjustment calculator to determine the amount the customer should pay for the current invoice.

§ Process payment using appropriate ‘Billing - Payments’ process.

 

3. Key F2 CSIN using the template below to request the rate rollback. Make sure to include:

·   Reason for cancellation request

·   Competitor name

·   Rate competitor quoted to our customer.

 

Rate Roll Back Effective Dates:

·   Close date end of current month (ex. 3/31/11)

·   New Rate effective date must be first day of following month (ex. 4/1/11)

Moving: OUT of Service Area

 

If the customer is moving outside division area then follow A - F:

 

     A. Obtain forwarding address and update account billing site.

 

     B. If invoice already paid in full beyond current month, key ADRF to request refund for over-payment (If applicable).

 

     C. If balance due, process payment using appropriate ‘Billing - Payments’ process.

 

     D. Ask if customer is enrolled in EBPP. Cancel any enrollment and/or scheduled payment from EBPP. 

 

     E. Ask the customer when their last pick up will be. Key CSIN to close the account the day AFTER the last service day.

 

     F. Provide new service area information from RepublicServices.com using the business locator, and offer to transfer customer to establish new service.

Moving: Inside Service Area

 

If customer is moving to an address within the same division:

 

  A. Review the Division Service Area page for pricing and retention tactics.

 

     B. Key new Residential Order Form and remember that the close date needs to be the day after the new location start date.

 

     C. Key CSIN on old account that we have submitted an order form to open new account, and close old account.

 

 

If customer will not allow us to setup new account:

 

     A. Obtain forwarding address and update account billing site.

 

     B. If invoice already paid in full beyond current month, key F2 ADRF to request refund for over-payment (If applicable).

 

     C. If balance due, process payment using appropriate ‘Billing - Payments’ process.

 

     D. Ask if customer is enrolled in EBPP. Cancel any enrollment and/or scheduled payment from EBPP. 

 

     E. Ask the customer when their last pick up will be. Key CSIN to close the account the day AFTER the last service day.

Existing Customer – TEMP COD

 

CS Associate / Specialist:

Get the following information and transfer the call to a CS Specialist Ext# 300017

  • Caller’s Name
  • Phone number
  • Account #
  • Division #

 

 

CS Consultant:

If customer has/had same size container, at the same site within last 4 months, follow the ‘Existing Customer: Direct Billed’ process.

 

Sell all COD requests that come into the center by following the ‘New Customer – Temp COD’ process unless:

-                             Large job which requires several boxes.

-                             Customer needs the container longer than Division Service Area states.

-                             Customer requesting better pricing due to amount of COD hauls needed.

-                             KMT states that we have to send these to Construction Rep.

 

COD applies until/unless credit is approved.

  •  If customer is requesting credit/billed CSR must transfer to Sales Rep to proceed with request, unless otherwise stated in KMT.
  • Credit Applications not provided by call center agents.

 

IF sending sale/call to Sales Rep, CSR does not fill out order form, data entry takes place at division.

 

If customer is requesting written quote send caller to Sales   DO NOT provide, email or fax written quotes.

 

Key SASQ on customers account to document that you flipped it to sales.

  • Account #
  • Division #

CHECK:

·         same size container, at the same site within last 4 months

·         Is current and up-to-date on ALL payments on their accounts.

·         KMT does NOT state to flip to division.

·         Complete Temp Order Form using same rates as previous/existing account information if the customer has or had.
