New Service: Quote
 
CS Associate / Specialist:

 

Get the following information and transfer the call to a CS Specialist Ext#300017

 

  • Caller’s Name
  • Phone number
  • Account #
  • Division #

    •  

     

    CS Consultant:

    Check:

    1.      Divisions quote account (#7777777) for existing quote within the current month.

    ·         If there is a quote in the divisions quote account, proceed to step D.

     

    2.      InfoPro for existing account (closed or open).

    • If OPEN account exists, verify if it’s the callers account.

    -             If it is NOT the caller’s account, proceed to step C

    • If CLOSED account exists, verify if it’s the callers account.

    -       If it is the caller’s account, and it has been closed in good standing, refer to Residential New Service: Sold

    -       If it is the caller’s account, and it has been closed due to bad debt or written off, If previous account exists and was written off refer to "Payment - Closed/Written Off" call handling

     

    Ask Probing Questions:

    ·         Has the caller been previously contacted by an [Allied Waste / Republic Services] representative concerning their waste needs?

    ·         Does the customer want services at more than one location?

    ·         Does the customer want 6YD trash, 8YD trash, or any recycling services?

    ·         Do they want a Perm Roll off or Compactor (any size)?

    ·         Are they a large construction company or job that will require multiple boxes or multiple hauls?

    ·         Is a site visit needed? (Needs quotes for multiple sizes/serv freq, low hanging wires, access to container, enclosure, etc...)

    ·         Do they want a Service Increase or Decrease?

     

       If 'YES' to ANY of these questions:

        -  Flip to sales rep (CALL SALES REP ON THEIR CELL PHONE). Leave a message if they do not answer and notate that in your F2.

        -  Enter an SASQ in 7777777 quote account

     

    If ‘NO’ to ALL of these questions:

       -  Continue to ask probing questions to determine business needs, and waste type. 

       -  Review Division Service Area page for additional pricing information if needed.

    • If you close the sale, follow the Commercial New Service: Sold process.

     

       If caller doesn’t commit to sign up for service:

       -  Log in pricing tool as FS

       -  Enter SASQ in 7777777 quote account

    Existing Customer – Direct Billed

    Get the following information and transfer the call to a CS Specialist Ext# 300017

    • Caller’s Name
    • Phone number
    • Account #
    • Division #

     

     

    CS Consultant:

    CHECK:

    ·         same size container, at the same site within last 4 months

    ·         Is current and up-to-date on ALL payments on their accounts.

    ·         KMT does NOT state to flip to division.

     

    If ALL of the above requirements are true:

    ·         Complete Temp Order Form using same rates as previous/existing account information if the customer has or had.

     

    If ANY of the above are not true:

    ·         Offer COD option to customer.

    §  Make sure to let the customer know they will have to pay up front for all SWG.

    §  If the customer wants COD option, follow the ‘New Customer – Temp COD’ process.

     

    Otherwise:

    ·         Flip sale; If voicemail required, leave detailed message

    ·         Provide the Sales Contact's name & phone number to customer

    ·         Key SASQ on customers account to document that you flipped it to sales.

     

    If customer is requesting written quote send caller to Sales   DO NOT provide, email or fax written quotes.

    Existing Customer – TEMP COD

     

    CS Associate / Specialist:

    Get the following information and transfer the call to a CS Specialist Ext# 300017

    • Caller’s Name
    • Phone number
    • Account #
    • Division #

     

     

    CS Consultant:

    If customer has/had same size container, at the same site within last 4 months, follow the ‘Existing Customer: Direct Billed’ process.

     

    Sell all COD requests that come into the center by following the ‘New Customer – Temp COD’ process unless:

    -                             Large job which requires several boxes.

    -                             Customer needs the container longer than Division Service Area states.

    -                             Customer requesting better pricing due to amount of COD hauls needed.

    -                             KMT states that we have to send these to Construction Rep.

     

    COD applies until/unless credit is approved.

    •  If customer is requesting credit/billed CSR must transfer to Sales Rep to proceed with request, unless otherwise stated in KMT.
    • Credit Applications not provided by call center agents.

     

    IF sending sale/call to Sales Rep, CSR does not fill out order form, data entry takes place at division.

     

    If customer is requesting written quote send caller to Sales   DO NOT provide, email or fax written quotes.

     

    Key SASQ on customers account to document that you flipped it to sales.
