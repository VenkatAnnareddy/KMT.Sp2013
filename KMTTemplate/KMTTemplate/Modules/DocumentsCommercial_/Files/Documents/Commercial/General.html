General – Account Changes

 

For billing address changes, make cosmetic change and key F2 CSIN

 

Only changes to names or streets can be made for misspellings or typos.

 

-  New site address requires a new site/container group.

  • Transfer call to Sales Rep and key SAIN

 

-  Ownership change requires a new account.

  • Transfer call to Sales Rep Key SAIN

 

(CALL SALES REP ON THEIR CELL PHONE). Leave a message if they do not answer and notate that in your F2.

 

F2 NOTE STANDARD

 

 

 

called to advise that business has been sold.  New owner's name is .  Please contact at .

General - Damage Claim - Container/ Property/Vehicle

 

Refer to Division Escalation contact found on the Division Information page:

 

  • Collect details and transfer and announce caller to Division contact person, and A or B:

 

A. If current customer, key F2 OPSD with details on customer account

 

B. If non-customer, email details to Division contact person

 

If container repair requested refer to 'Service - Container Repair' call handling process

General - W-9 Information - Tax ID

 

If our company invoices caller, transfer and announce caller or leave voice mail to Accounts Receivable Clerk.

-          Key closed CSIN on account.

-          F2 note not required since there is no account in InfoPro.
