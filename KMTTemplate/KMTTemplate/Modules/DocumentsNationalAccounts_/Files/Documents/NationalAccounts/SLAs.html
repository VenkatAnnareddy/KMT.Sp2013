National Accounts Reference Information:          
          
 ▪ National Accounts Customer Service requests from the divisions should be directed to NASSC.CustSvc@republicservices.com         
 ▪ Container overweight after disposal - when a compactor is overweight communication should be sent to National Accounts Customer service via nassc.custsvc@republicservices.com.  Include the customer name, date, location, plant/store number, the weight, our division name and number and a division contact person.           
          
          
 ▪ Container overweight before it is hauled- If a compactor cannot be hauled due to being overweight, the division should call 1-800-840-5409 to advise National Account customer service of this issue. Customer Service will contact the customer and advise them of the need to have the compactor off loaded.  National Accounts will coordinate that with the division or third party vendor to off-load.         
          
          
          
 ▪ Contaminated loads should be reported to the NECOInnovation@republicservices.com the message should include pictures of the contaminated loads, dates, account numbers, and any other relevant information.         
          
 ▪ Service Level Agreements are a part of the contractual agreements between Republic Services and the client. Corporate Operations is involved in discussions involving service level agreements that are outside of our service delivery standards.         
          
          
 ▪ National Accounts Pricing is done through the Corporate Pricing team and includes the field divisions. Final decisions are made at the corporate level and involve Republic Services competitive strategies.         
          
 ▪ National Account new customers are setup in Infopro by the National Accounts team, this includes the closure of existing accounts that would transition to the new clients portfolio           
          
          
          
                                                                                                                                                                                  All times are location local times
